Methodology

Correctness (WER)
Word Error Rate measures transcription accuracy using the formula: (Substitutions + Insertions + Deletions) / Total Words.
Naturalness
Algorithmic assessment of voice clarity using signal processing to detect artifacts and noise in generated audio.
Prosody
Evaluation of rhythm, stress, and intonation patterns critical for conveying emotion and meaning.
Speed (RTF)
Real-Time Factor: processing time divided by audio duration. RTF of 0.5 means 1s of audio takes 0.5s to generate.
Robustness
Performance consistency on challenging inputs including complex terminology and irregular text structures.
